Events by Rotaract Club of Pune Heritage

Our events range from service drives and General Meetings to workshops, fellowships, district initiatives, and collaborations across Pune. Some events are about giving back, some are about learning, and some are simply about bringing people together. Through each one, RCPH tries to create spaces where young people can lead, participate, and feel connected.
