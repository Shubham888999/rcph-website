About Rotaract Club of Pune Heritage

Chartered in 2015, the Rotaract Club of Pune Heritage was sponsored by the Rotary Club of Pune Heritage, RID 3131.

Our Charter President, Rtr. Parth Jaokar, exemplified leadership by serving as the District Rotaract Representative (DRR) of RI District 3131 for the Rotary Year 2021-22.

Since inception, the club has been known for its strong leadership, impactful service, and vibrant fellowship.

Our Projects

  • Mahadaan Drive: Collected 30 bags of clothes, 151 kg of foodgrains, and ₹21,362 cash to benefit two local societies.
  • Night School Visit: Hosted stationery distributions and interactive sessions with underprivileged children first of many warm, encouraging evenings.
  • Pyaas 2.0 Sherbet Drive: Served chilled sherbet to police officers and pedestrians under the May sun, bringing refreshment and smiles to every hand reached.
  • The Ice Breaker Meetup: Sparked new friendships with fun games and conversations with prospective members left as friends, excited for what’s next.
