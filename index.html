Rotaract Club of Pune Heritage in Pune (RID 3131)

Rotaract Club of Pune Heritage is a youth - led community service and leadership club in Pune under Rotaract District 3131, Zone 4. We plan and execute projects across community service, professional development, club service, and international service - inviting students and young professionals to create impact and network with like - minded leaders. See our Rotaract Pune events calendar, explore Rotaract projects in Pune, and join Rotaract in Pune.

Highlight of the Month

District Sports Meet Outdoor

DSMO was a high-octane sports tournament hosted in collaboration with Rotaract Club of Pune Mideast and Rotaract Club of Pune Warje. We brought together Rotaractors from across the district to compete, connect, and showcase their athletic skills. From cricket pitches to football fields, the energy was unmatched as we celebrated sportsmanship and unity.

Featured Projects

Pages of Hope

A book donation drive initiated by RC SSPU and collaborated with RC Pune Heritage. RCPH donated 20+ books to Ramabai Ranade Proudh High School with a small interaction session.

Inside Out

We successfully hosted Inside Out, an online Mental Health Awareness session in collaboration with Rotaract Club of Pune Metro and Rotaract Club of MGMIMSR Panvel. The event marked World Mental Health Day with impactful conversations on empathy, wellness, and awareness.

Project EduReach

The Rotaract Club of Pune Heritage undertook the distribution of 50 exclusive e-learning kits to 10th Std SSC students. This initiative was designed to provide the necessary academic support and to motivate students as they prepare for their board examinations, ensuring they have better access to learning resources and encouragement to achieve success.
