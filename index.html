About Rotaract Club of Pune Heritage

Chartered in 2015, the Rotaract Club of Pune Heritage was sponsored and established by the Rotary Club of Pune Heritage, RID 3131.

Our Charter President, Rtr. Parth Jaokar, exemplified leadership by serving as the District Rotaract Representative (DRR) of RI District 3131 for the Rotary Year 2021-22.

Since inception, the club has been known for its strong leadership, impactful service, and vibrant fellowship.
