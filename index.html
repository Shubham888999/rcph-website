Rotaract Club of Pune Heritage in Pune

Rotaract Club of Pune Heritage, or RCPH, is a community-based Rotaract club in Pune under Rotaract District 3131, Zone 4. We bring together students and young professionals who want to do more than just attend events - we plan projects, learn together, build friendships, and serve the community.

From education drives and awareness sessions to fellowships, district events, professional development activities, and collaborations with Rotary, RCPH gives members a space to create, connect, and contribute in a meaningful way. If you are looking to volunteer, collaborate, or join a youth-led service club in Pune, start with our Join page, explore our projects, or read the RCPH FAQ.

Featured Projects

Here are a few initiatives that show what RCPH is about - learning, service, fellowship, and small efforts that create real memories. You can explore more stories on our Projects page.

Pages of Hope

A book donation drive initiated by RC SSPU and collaborated with RC Pune Heritage. Rotaract Club of Pune Heritage donated 20+ books to Ramabai Ranade Proudh High School with a small interaction session.

Inside Out

We successfully hosted Inside Out, an online Mental Health Awareness session in collaboration with Rotaract Club of Pune Metro and Rotaract Club of MGMIMSR Panvel. The event marked World Mental Health Day with impactful conversations on empathy, wellness, and awareness.

Project EduReach

The Rotaract Club of Pune Heritage undertook the distribution of 50 exclusive e-learning kits to 10th Std SSC students. This initiative was designed to provide the necessary academic support and to motivate students as they prepare for their board examinations, ensuring they have better access to learning resources and encouragement to achieve success.
