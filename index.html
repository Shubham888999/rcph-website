Highlight of the Month

Mahadaan 2025

Mahadaan is a community donation drive in collaboration with Rotary Club of Pune Heritage to collect food, clothes, and funds for those in need. The event driven by our Community Service Director, Rtr. Nupura Danait, brings people together to share and support, showing how small contributions can create a big impact.
