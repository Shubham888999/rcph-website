Join Rotaract in Pune

Join Rotaract Club of Pune Heritage

Rotaract Club of Pune Heritage welcomes students and young professionals who want to create impact, connect with people and contribute to community service in Pune under RID 3131.

Create Member Account Ask a Question

Who Can Join?

If you are a student, young professional, volunteer, creator or changemaker in Pune, you can explore membership with RCPH. Rotaract is built for young adults who want hands-on service, leadership and fellowship.

Why Join RCPH?

Community Service

Work on meaningful projects with schools, communities, NGOs and local partners in Pune.

Professional Development

Learn through sessions, team leadership, public speaking, project planning and real responsibility.

Events and Fellowship

Attend Rotaract events Pune, GBMs, cultural exchanges, flagship experiences and district activities.

How to Apply or Volunteer

Start by creating a member account or contacting us. New GBM members can create an account and follow public updates, while BOD and admin access remains approval-based.

For collaboration, volunteering or membership questions, visit the Contact page.
