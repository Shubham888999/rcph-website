Join Rotaract in Pune

Join Rotaract Club of Pune Heritage

Rotaract Club of Pune Heritage welcomes students and young professionals who want to create impact, connect with people and contribute to community service in Pune under RID 3131.

Create Member Account Ask a Question

Who Can Join?

If you are a student or young professional in Pune and want to be part of service projects, events, friendships, and leadership opportunities, you can explore membership with RCPH. Rotaract is for people who want to learn outside the classroom and contribute beyond their routine.

Why Join RCPH?

Community Service

Work on meaningful projects with schools, communities, NGOs and local partners in Pune.

Professional Development

Learn through sessions, team leadership, public speaking, project planning and real responsibility.

Events and Fellowship

Attend our Rotaract events, General Body Meetings, cultural exchanges, flagship experiences and district activities in Pune.

How to Apply or Volunteer

You can start by creating a member account or by contacting us first if you have questions. Club members can create an account directly.

For membership, volunteering, or collaboration questions, visit the Contact page. You can also read the RCPH FAQ for quick answers about the club, membership, and website access.
